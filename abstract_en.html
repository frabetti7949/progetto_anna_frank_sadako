Voices in the Silence

A school project about Anne Frank and Sadako Sasaki.

Introduction

This project was created by our school group. We wanted to talk about two important girls: Anne Frank and Sadako Sasaki. Even if they lived in different places, they both show us how brave children can be during a war.

Our Goals

We created this website to remember their stories. We studied Anne's Diary and Sadako’s legend of the 1,000 paper cranes. We want to share a message of peace and hope with everyone.

"We believe that memory is important to build a better future without violence."

How we worked

First, we did research in books and online. Then, we chose the best photos and wrote the texts. Finally, we built this website. We also included a special part about a modern comic book (graphic novel) to show Sadako's story in a new way.
